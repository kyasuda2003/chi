New Product Line

After thorough research Pacific Oasis Enterprise, Inc. had learned that because of population growth, illness due to MISHANDLING of food items has become a fast growing problem o f Food Service Industries. Restaurants and fast food chains are estimating that the cost of payment claims and lost revenues has been nearly $100 Million in payment claims. Thus, "FOOD CODE BG 94-1" was passed to protect the food services indusutry.

FOOD CODE BG 94-1 prohibits food employees from contacting exposed, ready -to-eat food with bare hands. For such reasons Pacific Oasis Enterprise, Inc. has added the following new products:

NON-WOVEN DISPOSABLE ITEMS (Gloves,Aprons,Headwear, Overalls)
DISPOSABLE POLYETHYLENE GLOVES
DISPOSABLE CUTLERIES
DURAMITT GLOVES
